Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor at the Institute for International Economic Studies (IIES) at Stockholm University.

I do research at the intersection of environmental economics, development, and international trade. My work focuses on the causes and consequences of land use change. I combine causal identification methods and spatial general equilibrium models to understand which conservation policies work — and at what economic cost.
